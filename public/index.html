About This Study

Background & Ethics

This research investigates the integration of security architectural design in places of worship in Nigeria, with focus on selected state capitals. Data collected through these instruments will inform academic publication and policy recommendations.

All responses are confidential. No personally identifying details about respondents will appear in published findings without explicit consent. Data is stored in a secure Google Sheet accessible only to the research team.
